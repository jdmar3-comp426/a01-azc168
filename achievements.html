Achilles' Achievements

Achilles is most well known for his involvement in the Trojan War. Due to his near invincibility, he had great success on the battlefield. In fact, he was undefeated although the war itself had reached a stalemate. On of Achilles key victory's was his defeat of Hector. When the Trojan Prince Hector killed Achilles's best friend Patroclus, Achilles vowed to take revenge. Thetis asked the divine blacksmith Hephaestus to make a sword and shield that would keep him safe. Achilles chased Hector back to Troy, slaughtering Trojans all the way. When they got to the city walls, Hector tried to reason with his pursuer, but Achilles was not interested. He stabbed Hector in the throat, killing him.
